* Lead AI and Machine Learning initiatives
* Conduct AI workshops and training sessions
* Mentor junior members
* Guide hackathon teams
* Review AI projects

* Python
* Machine Learning

* LLMs
* RAG
* LangChain
* Leadership
* Communication

## Member Selection Rules

1. Prefer members with relevant skills.
2. Prefer members with previous event participation.
3. Prefer members with leadership experience.
4. Prefer members with hackathon achievements.
5. Prefer active members over inactive members.

---

## Recommendation Criteria

Score Members Based On:

* Skills Match (40%)
* Leadership (20%)
* Participation (20%)
* Achievements (20%)

Highest score should be recommended.
